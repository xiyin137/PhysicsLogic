## Reading Summary
- Extracted classical bosonic OSFT BV framework: open-string field/antifield
  splitting and dual bases, odd symplectic pairing, cyclic open-string
  vertices from boundary moduli chains, geometric master equation for chains,
  normalization recursion, and field redefinition conventions (`osftdualbas`,
  `closftactbv`, `cyclicopenvert`, `geomopen`, `nnnopen`,
  `opsftphaseredef`).
- Extracted equation/gauge structure: OSFT EOM with open-string brackets,
  `A_infinity` relations, and gauge transformations generated by `Q_Psi`
  (`osftainfeom`, `ainftyrel`).
- Extracted Witten cubic OSFT construction from quadratic differentials:
  strip decomposition, cubic action, star-product definition, associativity,
  and cubic gauge transformations (`horcond`, `minmetricosft`, `cubictrans`,
  `cubicbv`, `fffsd`, `hzdefz`, `osfteq`, `osftgaugev`).
- Extracted tachyon-condensation evidence in level truncation: tachyon trial
  ansatz, cubic potential evaluation, and progression toward full tension
  cancellation with higher-level truncations (`psitrial`, `cuopes`).
- Extracted wedge-state and `KBc` algebra technology: wedge semigroup under
  star product, sliver-frame representation, and `K,B,c` algebraic relations
  used for analytic solutions (`omegaabadd`, `omegauf`, `conewedgeid`,
  `slivermaps`, `kbca`, `kbcb`).
- Extracted analytic tachyon-vacuum solution and consequences: explicit
  `KBc` solution, EOM verification, trivial `Q_{Psi_tv}` cohomology via
  homotopy operator, and exact D-brane-tension cancellation (`tvkbc`,
  `qahomtriv`, `ckktem`).
- Extracted more general bosonic OSFT solutions:
  Kiermaier-Okawa marginal-deformation solutions (integrable and regularized
  nonintegrable OPE cases), reality recovery via gauge dressing, and the free
  boson `cos(X/sqrt(alpha'))` deformation with boundary-state interpolation
  (`pertosftsoex`, `koksecsol`, `qbcseondorderv`, `psilallord`,
  `psilallordr`, `uunregdef`, `psirealrecov`, `deforintval`,
  `qblambvvreg`, `vmargcosx`, `bsutworota`).
- Extracted rolling-tachyon revisit in OSFT: time-dependent solution
  construction, covariant phase-space/symplectic prescription with midpoint
  projection regularization, and energy extraction matching worldsheet and
  Ellwood-invariant data (`bdryfg`, `bdryfgexp`, `dthetadef`,
  `omegasympwitfix`, `omegafirstord`).
- Extracted Erler-Maccaferri background-independence solutions: intertwining
  construction between BCFT backgrounds, homotopy/identity constraints,
  flag-state realization, and multi-brane constructions from a single-brane
  OSFT (`psiintertw`, `qsigmapsi`, `sigmasigmaone`, `eqahomot`,
  `sigmapreaa`, `sigmamasgder`, `sigmabsig`, `psitmgeng`).
- Extracted BV formulation of quantum open+closed bosonic SFT:
  combined field space, genus/boundary string vertices, exceptional disc
  one-point vertex treatment, open+closed geometric master relations, and
  hyperbolic-vertex conditions including Witten-cubic limit (`oncact`,
  `oncvert`, `omegaopenclsft`, `disconv`, `qbopclsf`).
- Extracted open+closed superstring field theory extension:
  NS/R open-field spaces with auxiliary Ramond-picture fields, BV pairing,
  full action and super vertices with PCO chains, linearized closed-string
  source equation from D-brane boundary state, and Siegel-gauge sourced
  profile when unobstructed (`psiexpansi`, `bvsftfnlsuperoc`,
  `disconeptsup`, `qbpsilinsup`, `qbpsilinsupsol`).
